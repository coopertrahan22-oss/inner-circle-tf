WELCOME TO
THE INNER CIRCLE

For over two years, Inner Circle Training Facility has been New Bedford's home for elite fitness. We built this space for people who are serious about results — whether you're an athlete looking to dominate your sport or someone ready to transform their body and mindset.

Our facility is stocked with top-of-the-line equipment and led by coaches who push you past limits you didn't know you had. Iron sharpens iron here — come join a community that will never let you settle.
